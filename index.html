According to the UNHCR, over 100 million Ukrainians have been displaced as a result of the Russian invasion.

Approximately 6 million Ukrainians are internally displaced. Their homes have been destroyed by bombings. Russians have taken over their cities and families have been forced to escape. Many still live close to the front lines and don't have the financial means to relocate to safer areas of the country.

After 2 years of war, civilians are still being bombed every day.

The need for humanitarian assistance is overwhelming.

Our Mission

The Ukraine War Relief Fund helps Ukrainian families who are trapped in dangerous combat zones by providing them new homes in safer areas of the country.

BASE UA

In collaboration with our Ukrainian partner organization, BASE UA, we purchase homes in secure regions of Ukraine and relocate our families to these homes where they will be safe from artiller fire.

Thank You

We are a small grassroots organization, passionate about helping Ukraine.

The need is desperate and immediate, and your donations will save lives.
